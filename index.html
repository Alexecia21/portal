About Me

Hello, my name is Alexecia Lim and welcome to my site. By the time you leave here you'll get to learn a little about me.

I currently am a full-time student at Year Up Puget Sound. I am an aspiring software developer who is highly motivated with a goal-driven work ethic. I embrace the lessons learned from failure and use it to continue to grow. In a nutshell, my passion for creativity and constant learning made me transition out of healthcare and into computer programming.

In my free time I enjoy working on self-development, building on my personal brand, taking care of my health, and going on walks with my dog. I am also a foodie who loves finding new restaurants and new adventures along the way.
